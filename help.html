How Can I Help You?

How can I search the product or service?
You can search for desirable products and services on the website by categories and with key words. Clicking on the button “Search” there will appear advertisements for you. On the left side of the search results there is additional menu which helps you to filter advertisements in details by price range, by product condition, by type of advertisement or by type of selling
How can I register on the website?
E-mail - In this field please indicate your active e-mail, because after registration it is necessary to activate the profile from the specified e-mail. Password - In the corresponding field please write a desirable combination of symbols. You can use Latin/Georgian letters, numbers, punctuation. Repeat the password Retype the indicated combination in the password box. Code - Enter picture symbols in the code field. After filling all the required fields above, you should view the web page terms and conditions, agree on it and click the button “Registration”. To activate your personal profile, you need to go to the activation link, which will be sent to the specified e-mail. If E-mail you specified is already have registered in MY.GE space, you can use existing profile, and if necessary, you can recover the password. After activating your profile, you can log in and use all Mymarket services.
How can I buy product or service on website?
In order to buy the products on Mymarket, you should login or register Enter the required product page, click on "Buy", select quantity of units and click on "Continue". In case of first purchase, you need to fill your personal information and delivery address. For that click on the button select "new address list", fill in the recipient's name, number, tel. Number and address where you want to receive the order. In case of, if you want to buy the product on behalf of a legal entity, you must indicate in the appropriate fields: name of the organization, identification code, telephone number, city (village) and detailed address where you want to receive the order. In case of repeated purchase, you can select previously added delivery address or, if necessary, add the new one. After selecting the address, click on the "Continue" button. Check the purchased item and choose one of the 5 payment methods: Card payment method - Select "Card Payment" from the payment methods, enter the card details and complete the purchase process. (In case of a legal entity, payment is made with a company card). Payment with Ertguli bonuses - Select "Payment with Ertguli bonuses" from the payment methods, enter the card details and complete the purchase process. Payment with smart payment machine - select "Payment with smart payment machine" from the payment methods, after that you receive the invoice number. In the smart payment machine, select Myshop, enter the invoice number and pay the specified amount. Space installment - Select "Space installment" from the payment methods, After clicking on payment, you will go to the page where you will find a QR code and you can easily scan it with the application. Space QR installment will be issued in the range of 100-25,000 GEL. This product can be used by anyone who is 20-60 years old and has a monthly income. TBC installment - Select "TBC installment" from the payment methods, After clicking on payment, you will go to the page where you will be filled all required fields and click “Send”. TBC installment will be issued in the range of 100-5,000 GEL. This product can be used by anyone who is 18-75 years old and has a monthly income. Payment for the cost of the product is also possible by bank transfer, for that, click on the "Invoice Request" button below the "Choose Payment Method" button and the invoice will be sent to the email you specified. Payment order is valid for one business day. When paying, it is necessary to indicate the invoice number.
